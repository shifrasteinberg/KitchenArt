Recipes: Purim


Cherry Hamantash Muffins

Here's a recipe that will give you that special Purim hamantash look, without rolling out any dough! They are made in silicone triangle baking cups; feel free to make them all year round in any shape.


Ingredients:


  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk or soy buttermilk (mix 1 tablespoon vinegar with enough milk to equal 3/4 cup and let stand 1 minute)
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups cherry pie filling

Directions:


Preheat the oven to 375°F. Lay out 12 triangle baking cups on a cookie sheet.

 

Mix all dry ingredients until well blended. Make a well in the center. Add the egg, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla extract and mix until just combined.

 

Spoon the batter into the baking cups, about one third of the way up. Mound one tablespoon of cherry pie filling in the center of the batter in each cup. Pipe the remaining batter around the edge of each muffin, forming a slight arc on each side.

 

Bake for 25-30 minutes. Yield: 12 muffins.




Good-For-You Muffins

Bake these healthy muffins in silly-feet silicone muffin cups, and your children will beg for more!


Ingredients:


  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup wheat germ
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded carrot
  • 1/2 cup coconut
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries or raisins
  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds, chopped pumpkin seeds or chopped pecans
  • 3/4 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup milk or soymilk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs

Optional Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1 cup icing sugar
  • 2-3 teaspoons apple juice or milk

Directions:


Preheat the oven to 350°F.

 

Combine the first seven ingredients in a large bowl. Add the apples, carrots, coconut, cranberries, and seeds or nuts; mix well. Add the remaining ingredients; stir just until dry ingredients are moistened.

 

Fill the muffin cups three quarters full.

 

Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

 

Beat the frosting ingredients until smooth. Spread on cooled muffins. Insert a clown head into each muffin, or decorate as desired.

 

Yield: 18-20 muffins
